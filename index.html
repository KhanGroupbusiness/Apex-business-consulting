How to Start a Business in Belgium

A practical overview of the key steps entrepreneurs should understand before launching their activity.

Clarify your activity

Define what you will offer, who your clients are, and whether your activity is service-based, retail, online, or consultancy.

Choose the right structure

Common options include sole proprietorship for simple freelance activity and company structures such as an SRL/BV for more separation and scalability.

Register correctly

You usually need an enterprise number, activation through an enterprise counter, and VAT registration where applicable.

Organize from day one

Keep contracts, invoices, bank records, supplier documents and client communication structured from the beginning.
